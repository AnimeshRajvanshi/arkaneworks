AX-1.0: Patriot

This is the Level 1 kit I assembled and certified with as part of my high-powered rocketry mission under Arkane Works. As an Amateur Rocket & Payload Engineer with the Tripoli Rocketry Association, I assembled the LOC Patriot rocket (4" diameter, 40" length) for Level 1 certification, demonstrating skills in epoxy bonding, through-the-wall fins, rail buttons, and motor retainers.

Integrated an Aerotech H100W-14A motor (227 Ns impulse) with timed delay and ~1.2g black powder ejection charge, conducting ground tests for parachute reliability. This build serves as a foundational platform for rocketry certification and experimentation.

Simulated flights in OpenRocket for stability margin (>1 caliber), apogee prediction, and CG/CP optimization. Used ANSYS Mechanical to test structural integrity under thrust.

Conducted CFD in Fluent for airflow analysis and high-speed heating. This project links to broader aerospace applications, including payload integration and recovery systems.
