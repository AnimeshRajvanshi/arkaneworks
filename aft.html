Aerondight Flight Technologies

A.F.T. is the flight-vehicle program that carries the AX rocket lineage forward. The near-term work is deliberate and unglamorous: airframe and recovery iteration, avionics bring-up, deployable-payload mechanisms, and the test discipline needed to fly progressively more capable vehicles without skipping steps. The program brief stops here on purpose — specifics will be published as hardware is built and flown, not before.
