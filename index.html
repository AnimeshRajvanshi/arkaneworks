Arkane Works is the independent engineering practice of Animesh Rajvanshi — mechanical engineer (B.S.M.E., Arizona State University; EIT). The work spans high-power rocketry, autonomous flight, spacecraft concepts, lunar infrastructure studies, and semiconductor metrology. Everything in the index was designed, simulated, built, or certified first-hand — and documented as it happened, including the parts that didn't work.
