Gorgon CubeSat

The Gorgon CubeSat, proposed at ASU’s Sun Devil Satellite Laboratory in 2023, is a 12U satellite designed to monitor ocean plastic pollution using hyperspectral imaging, with a novel Starlink-based communication system.

Satellite Showcase

[Placeholder: Add additional renders or videos here]

I collaborated on subsystem integration, specifying components like Blue Canyon XACT-50 ADCS and DHV Technology solar panels, and independently proposed a modified Starlink terminal for high-bandwidth data relay.

The CubeSat targets a 500 km orbit, using a SWIR imager (1000-1700 nm, 50 m GSD) to detect macroplastics, addressing the global “Missing Plastic Problem” with innovative space-based monitoring.

Though conceptual, this project showcases my skills in system design, thermal management, and creative problem-solving, applicable to advanced aerospace engineering challenges.
