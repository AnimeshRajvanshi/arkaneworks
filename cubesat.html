Gorgon CubeSat

The Gorgon CubeSat is a compact satellite designed to demonstrate advanced imaging and communication technologies in low Earth orbit, built during a university aerospace initiative.

This 3U CubeSat integrates a custom payload for high-resolution Earth observation, with my contributions focusing on the thermal management system to protect sensitive electronics in space.

The project showcases my skills in spacecraft design, balancing size, weight, and power constraints while ensuring operational reliability in the vacuum of space.
