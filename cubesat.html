Ocean Sentinel CubeSat

From July 2021 to July 2022, I managed the Hyperspectral CubeSat project at ASU’s Sun Devil Satellite Laboratory, leading a team of engineering students to design a CubeSat with a hyperspectral camera aimed at cataloging ocean plastic pollution. I established the scientific mission, delegated tasks like camera integration and structural design, and revamped our sponsorship package to secure funding. I also supported the lab’s board by managing the workshop, planning outreach events, and ensuring resource allocation, delivering a small satellite with a bold environmental vision.
