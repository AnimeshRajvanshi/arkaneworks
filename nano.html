Nano-Probe Vanguard

Since May 2024, I’ve researched nanoelectronics metrology and failure analysis at ASU’s Celano Lab, focusing on chiplets from top industry vendors. I implement scanning acoustic microscopy (SAM) to detect flaws and use CIVA NDT software to simulate these analyses, ensuring precision in defect identification. I’ve created detailed CAD models for SAM simulations and 3D assets for research papers, while conducting literature reviews on Cu-Cu hybrid bonding and failure modes in advanced 3D packaging. This work advances semiconductor reliability for cutting-edge tech.
