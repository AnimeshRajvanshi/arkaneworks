Stormveil UAV Systems

Stormveil (S.U.S.) is Arkane Works' active program in small autonomous aircraft. It is early. The work so far is software and simulation — early flight-control and autonomy prototypes — with hardware design moving in parallel. Nothing has flown yet, and the direction is kept deliberately limited while the work matures. This page is short on purpose; it will grow as real, repeatable milestones are met.
