Lunar Power Grid

The Lunar Power Grid project explores the feasibility of establishing a sustainable energy infrastructure on the Moon, designed to support long-term human presence and scientific operations.

Developed as part of a conceptual study, this system integrates solar arrays optimized for the lunar day-night cycle, energy storage solutions, and distribution networks tailored to the Moon's harsh environment.

The project leverages my experience in systems engineering to address challenges like extreme temperature swings, regolith abrasion, and the 14-day light-dark periods, ensuring a reliable power supply for habitats and rovers.
