Lunar Power Grid

For NASAâ€™s Watts on the Moon Challenge, my team and I developed a power delivery solution for lunar operations, adapting a JAXA-Toyota Lunar Cruiser rover to lay cables across the lunar surface. I helped design a proprietary cable-laying mechanism, inspired by electric dog collar trenchers, to connect power plants to modified lunar landers serving as battery packs. We incorporated space-grade docking connectors to prevent contamination and added corrosion-resistant coatings to the rover and cables to withstand abrasive lunar sand, ensuring reliable energy distribution for Artemis missions.
