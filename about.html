Profile.01

Animesh Rajvanshi

Looking up at the stars, I have for long believed that these astronomical objects have been put in our vision's confines for a reason. Combined with the belief that as humans our role in nature is to build technology, I took to engineering to help me contribute to this grand design.

I am a graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering. Having always fostered a growth mindset as one of my prime directives, I have dedicated myself to learning information and creating systems to meaningfully contribute to our world. Being in a field where things are prioritized over people, I still love humanity and value relationships — and as a result, even though I have never shied away from undertaking solo projects, I thrive in collaborative environments.

With an affinity for problem solving — which is why I gravitate to Jiu Jitsu as my foremost hobby — and a commitment to pushing boundaries, I'm excited to shape the future of engineering. And someday find the answer to the great question: "Why is there something rather than nothing?"
