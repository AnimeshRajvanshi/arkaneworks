About Me

Hey there! I’m a Mechanical Engineering student at Arizona State University, passionate about tackling complex challenges through innovative design and technology. My journey spans aerospace systems, environmental solutions, and community-focused engineering, with projects ranging from lunar power grids to ocean plastic monitoring.

I thrive on blending creativity with technical rigor, drawing from hands-on experience in CAD, simulation tools, and interdisciplinary teamwork. At ASU, I’ve contributed to student-led initiatives like the Sun Devil Satellite Laboratory and EPICS, while pursuing independent research to push engineering boundaries.

Arkane Works is my space to showcase these efforts—each project reflects a commitment to solving real-world problems with practical, forward-thinking solutions. Thanks for stopping by!
